Field of Interest

I love to code, whether it be solving problems or just creating cool things with it. At the moment I am more geared towards front end development for websites and applications. However, I am aspiring to become a full stack developer.


Why coding?

I was introduced to HTML and CSS in high school, and I instantly feel in love with it. I also had a small experience with C# as I made a game for my final major project. From then on, I have become increasingly curious with coding, focussing more on Javascript and have gradually improved my skills.

I find that coding is very rewarding for me as it allows me to bring my ideas to life in a way that can be experienced and interacted with by others. There have been countless times where I have run into errors that have been difficult to overcome, but the sheer joy and satisfaction that I feel when I finally complete a project, and being proud of the outcome, is what has kept me following the coding trail.
